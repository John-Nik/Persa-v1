About Persa

Hello, I'm Persa, a passionate and dedicated dietitian with a mission to empower individuals to achieve their health and wellness goals. With a background in nutritional science and a wealth of practical experience, I'm here to guide you on your journey to better health.

I have studied nutritional science for 4 years, and have had well over a decade worth of experience in this field, with countless of patients helped to achieve their health goals.

I'm a firm believer in the power of education and motivation, and I'm dedicated to helping you make lasting, positive changes. Together, we'll create a sustainable, personalized plan that will bring you closer to your health and wellness goals. Let's embark on this journey to better living together.
